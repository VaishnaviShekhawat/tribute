Here's a time-line of his life

    • 1931:Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.

    • 1954:Graduted in Physics from University of Madras.

    • 1960:Graduated, Aerospace Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology.

    • 1961:Joined DRDO as a scientist.

    • 1969:Joined space Research at ISRO.

    • 1980:Become the project Director for India's First Indegenous Satellite.

    • 1981:Awarded Padma Bhushan.

    • 1990:Awarded Padma Vibhushan.

    • 1992:Chief Advisor for the India's Nuclear program.

    • 1997:Bharat Ratna,Indira Gandhi Award for National Integration.

    • 1998:Veer Savarkar Award.

    • 2000:Ramanujan Award.

    • 2002:Become 11th President of India.

    • 2015:The people President passed away while addressing the students.
